WELCOME TO CMSDI

Cmsdi Food Services Private Limited incorporated in May 2015. Cmsdi Foods is an initiative by the BTW Group (est. 2011), focusing on providing food services. With immense experience in the food industry, Cmsdi Foods are a leading exporter of all kinds of Indian spices, pulses, grains, Fruits, Vegetables, and many more, to a number of destinations around the globe. With several partnerships with buyers across multiple countries, we have grown to be among the go-to companies for food export from India.
